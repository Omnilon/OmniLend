Frequently Asked Questions

1. What types of credit do you accept?

We accept all types of credit, whether you have good credit, bad credit, or no credit at all. Our financing options are designed to help everyone get on the road.

2. How long does the approval process take?

Our approval process is quick and straightforward. It typically takes about 5 minutes to complete the application and receive a decision.

3. What are the minimum income requirements?

You need to have a minimum income of $800 per month to qualify for our financing options.

4. Do you require a down payment?

We offer down payments as low as $39! Our team will work with you to find a payment plan that fits your budget.

5. Can I trade in my current motorcycle?

Yes, we accept trade-ins! Bring your current motorcycle, and we'll provide a fair appraisal to help lower the cost of your new purchase.

6. What brands of motorcycles do you offer?

We carry a wide selection of high-quality motorcycles from top brands such as Harley-Davidson, Honda, Yamaha, Suzuki, and more.

7. Do you offer warranties on your motorcycles?

Yes, we offer warranties on many of our motorcycles. Our sales team can provide detailed information on warranty options available for each model.

8. How can I schedule a test ride?

To schedule a test ride, please contact us via phone or email, or visit our showroom during business hours. We'll be happy to arrange a convenient time for you.

9. What are your business hours?

Our business hours are Monday through Saturday, 9:00 AM to 7:00 PM. We are closed on Sundays.
